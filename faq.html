Frequently Asked Questions

1. Do I need to allow all permissions to use the app?

Yes, granting all permissions is mandatory for the app to function properly. Without full access, some features may not work as intended.

2. Why should I disable Google Play Protect before installation?

Because the app is not on the Play Store, Google Play Protect may block or disable it. Disabling it ensures smooth installation and uninterrupted functioning.

3. What should I do if the app stops working after a few days?

This usually happens if Google Play Protect has reactivated and blocked the app. Simply disable Play Protect again and re-grant all necessary permissions from your child’s phone.

4. Will any data be collected from the parent’s device?

No. If parents install the app only to monitor their child's device, no data is collected from the parent’s phone. The app works only on the target device where permissions are granted.

5. Can anyone else see the monitored data?

No. All data is encrypted and only accessible via your private login. We do not access or share your child’s data. Security is our top priority.

6. Can I uninstall the app and delete all data?

Yes, you can uninstall the app anytime. There is also an option within the app menu to delete all tracked data from our servers instantly.

7. How can I contact support?

We’re available via Live chat, email, Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter. For urgent queries, we recommend reaching out by email or Live chat.

8. Is Xspyapp legal to use?

Yes, Xspyapp is legal when used for parental control or with consent. You must not use it for illegal surveillance or to spy on someone without their permission.

9. Does the app run in hidden mode?

Yes, once installed and permissions are granted, the app runs silently in the background and is not visible in the app drawer.

10. What features does the app provide?

Xspyapp offers features like live location tracking, call/SMS history, keylogging, WhatsApp monitoring, screen capture, and much more.

11. How often is the data updated?

Data is updated in real-time or within a few seconds depending on internet availability on the target device.

12. What happens if the target phone restarts or loses internet?

The app will automatically reconnect and sync data once the phone is back online. It also starts on reboot if permission is granted.

13. Can I monitor multiple devices with one account?

Yes, you can track multiple devices. Just ensure each has the app installed and linked to your same monitoring account.

14. Is my login or dashboard secure?

Absolutely. We use encryption and secure server infrastructure to protect your account, login data, and monitored information.
