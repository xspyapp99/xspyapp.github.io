Privacy Policy – Xspyapp

We understand how important your privacy and trust are. Xspyapp is committed to protecting the data and digital security of all users. This policy clearly outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect information, with a special focus on protecting the rights of guardians and parents who use this application to safeguard their children.

1. What Data Do We Collect?

1.1 User-Provided Data: When you install and register the app, we may collect your email, device name, and selected settings – used only for account and support purposes.

1.2 Automatically Collected Data: Device ID, IP address, mobile OS version, usage logs, crash reports – used to ensure functionality and improve services.

2. Is Guardian Data Safe?

Absolutely Yes. Guardians who use this app to track their children are never tracked themselves. The Xspyapp only collects data from the device where all permissions are granted (i.e., the child's device). Your personal mobile or login device is never monitored, and we never access, share, or store the parent's personal data.

3. Is the Tracked Data Secure?

All tracked data is end-to-end encrypted and accessible only by the parent/guardian via secure login. No third party – including our team – can read or use this data. Without your login credentials, no one can view your dashboard or reports.

4. Do You Sell or Share My Data?

Never. Xspyapp does not share, sell, rent, or transfer any data to third-party advertisers or companies. Your data is strictly used to power the services you have requested.

5. What if I Uninstall the App?

After uninstallation, all data is automatically removed from our servers within 7 days. You may also manually delete all tracking data anytime from within your dashboard settings.

6. What About Third-Party Services?

We may use secure and trusted tools such as Firebase, AdMob, or Google Analytics to maintain performance – these services have their own privacy policies and do not access your private tracked content.

7. Guardian Control Rights

  • Guardians can uninstall or delete data anytime.
  • Guardians control when and how tracking happens.
  • No action is taken on child’s device without their full consent (via permissions).

8. Security Measures

We use SSL encryption, secure firewalls, login monitoring, and strict internal policies to protect your account and data. Our servers are hosted in secure environments compliant with international data protection laws.

9. Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated occasionally. When we make changes, we’ll notify users via our website. Continued usage means agreement to the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about your privacy, contact us at info@xspyapp.com
